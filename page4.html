Story of a king

Long, long before the world began, there was a king, the King of glory. This King was far, far above and beyond anyone or anything you or I could imagine. In the endlessness of eternity He was the only King, and His kingdom the only kingdom, a realm of perfect wisdom, love, joy, and peace. The kingdom had no need of sun or stars, for the King Himself was its light. While the kingdom was limitless in its size, it was limited in its subjects. Some say the King had no subjects at all. Or did He?
